New Business

In order to function well as a team and to clearly know how we will proceed, we need to decide on a team contract with the rules that we will each acknowledge and abide by. We should also consider how we want to brand ourselves. This includes choosing a well-fitting name and a theme for our group. People will recognize us solely off of these things, so whatever we choose needs to pop. Now concerning the development of the project, we will break up the project into modules. We'll strive to complete each module by a certain deadline.
